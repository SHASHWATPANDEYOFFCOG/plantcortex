The knowledge cliff

A plant's memory is scattered — and walking out the door.

Siloed

Six formats, zero links

A drawing, a spreadsheet, a PDF and a scan each hold a piece of the same asset. Nothing connects P-101A across them.

Slow

Minutes per question

Answers mean opening folders and Ctrl-F across systems. Multi-document questions are effectively unanswerable.

Fading

Tacit expertise retires

The senior operator who knows the pump "throws its seal when the bearing gets noisy" leaves — and the insight leaves with them.

"Chat with your PDFs" doesn't solve this. The knowledge has to become a structured, cross-modal, causal model.

Novelties N2 & N3 · retrieve, then anticipate

From answering questions to warning before failure.

N2 · Hippocampal retrieval

Multi-hop over a multimodal graph

Personalized PageRank seeds from typed query entities — or from a photographed equipment tag in the field, not just typed text. Joins facts across documents a vector search can't reach.

N3 · From answering to anticipating

Learned causal edges

A miner discovers that vibration precedes seal leakage on P-101A and writes a HAS_CAUSE edge. A new work order is then met with its probable cause, ranked from history.

Knowledge Capture — the heart of it

A retiring operator records a voice note → transcribed, entity-linked, and instantly a citable source. The engineer retires. The knowledge doesn't.
