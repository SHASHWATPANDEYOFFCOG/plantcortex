This is Ramesh, senior operator, Unit 2. A word on feed charge pump P-101A. Whenever you hear the DE bearing getting noisy and you see the vibration climbing, that mechanical seal is about to go. We have lost the seal on P-101A three or four times over the years, and every single time the vibration warned us a few weeks before it leaked. So please trend the vibration on P-101A, and if it starts rising, plan a seal change at the next shutdown instead of waiting for it to fail.
